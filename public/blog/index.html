Build vs Buy

Aug 13, 2019

I have a bias to buy rather than build. I have this bias primarily because when buying is even an option I can then infer that there’s a market for that product. So I have to ask: “Is my core business competing in that market?” If the answer is “No,” that is, if my core business isn’t in the market of products like the one being considered for build, then building is hard to justify.
